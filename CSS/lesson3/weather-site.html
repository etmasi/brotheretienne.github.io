Site Name

The name of the organizational site will be www.yourweather.edu and the domain for the site will be called www.yourweather.edu.

Site Purpose

The focus of www.yourweather.edu will be to help inform and educate users about the weather conditions in a specific location. In addition to providing weather predictions to specified locations, www.yourWeather.com will also issue rigour weather warnings and information opportunities for the local communities. The site will provide daily forecasts that include hourly weather forecasts. furthermore, extended week long forecasts will be provided so that the local population may be able to plan ahead the community activities. Weather-related notifications will also be posted to the site as well as the most recent weather-related reports and a gallery of weather-related pictures for use of the communities
